    Ktory - Dzisaj Jest ... - Today is...

    Dzisaj jest... means 'today is...'.

    The correct format for saying this is:

    1st January 1993
    Nominative (ordinal) Genitive NominativeGenitive
    pierwszy stycznia NominativeGenitive
